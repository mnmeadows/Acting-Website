February 2023

Cast in the ensemble of The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Nighttime, by Simon Stephens. Marissa will portray Voice One, Mrs. Shears, Mrs. Gascoyne, Woman on Train, and Shopkeeper in Lindenwood University's production to be directed by Emily Jones. Marissa is also the projections designer for this production.

SDecember 2022

Working again with Magic Smoking Monkey Theatre, Marissa was in the ensemble of It's A Marvelous Life, directed by Donna Northcott. She portrayed Mr. Gower, Loki, Black Widow, and Spiderman, working with puppets for multiple roles as well.

May 2022

In her first professional production working outside of the University, Marissa worked in the ensemble of Magic Smoking Monkey Theatre's production of Jurassic Park: The Musical, a musical parody directed by Donna Northcott.

April 2022

Played Bethany in Beauty, by Jane Martin, directed by student Jalyn Jordan in Lindenwood University's Spring Play Fest.

February 2022

Ensemble of Dead Man's Cell Phone, by Sarah Ruhl, added in by director by John O'Hagan.

October 2021

Played Cathy in Lindenwood University's fall production of You On The Moors Now, by Jaclyn Backhaus, directed by Donna Northcott.

February 2021

Portrayed Saranda in Kurt Gerard Heinlein's Evangeline Drowning, directed by Lindenwood University's John O'Hagan.
