August 2023

Cast as a Martian Ambassador and Christmas Caroler for Main Street of St. Charle's: Legend's and Lanterns, and Christmas Traditions festivals.

May 2023

Participated in Tin Roof's Seven/24 Play Festival where we produced a play from the ground up in 24 hours. Marissa acted in the 10 minute play Press Play, directed by Chris Grega, which one first place by audience choice.

February 2023

Cast in The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Nighttime, by Simon Stephens, as Voice One, Mrs. Shears, Mrs. Gascoyne, Woman on Train, and Shopkeeper. Directed by Emily Jones at Lindenwood University. Marissa was also the projections designer for this production.

December 2022

Working again with Magic Smoking Monkey Theatre, Marissa was in the ensemble of It's A Marvelous Life, directed by Donna Northcott. She portrayed Mr. Gower, Loki, Black Widow, and Spiderman, working with puppets for multiple roles as well.

May 2022

In her first professional production working outside of the University, Marissa worked in the ensemble of Magic Smoking Monkey Theatre's production of Jurassic Park: The Musical, a musical parody directed by Donna Northcott.

April 2022

Played Bethany in Beauty, by Jane Martin, directed by student Jalyn Jordan in Lindenwood University's Spring Play Fest.

February 2022

Ensemble of Dead Man's Cell Phone, by Sarah Ruhl, added in by director by John O'Hagan.
