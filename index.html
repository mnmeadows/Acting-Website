I grew up in Monument, Colorado where I started out doing theatre summer camps before slowly letting it take over all of my time in high school.

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Acting at Lindenwood University in St. Charles, Missouri.

I am also working on getting my graphic design minor (that hard work can be seen here with this website that I built from scratch).

Any spare time that I have left is spent trying to improve my drawing and piano skills.
